PRODUCT DETAIL

Shooting Modes

Fotomat has two shooting modes: Automatic and Bulb modes to choose from, meaning that you can take perfectly exposed shots in any and every shooting situation.

Built-in 35mm lens

The lens has an equivalent focal length of 35mm, so it’s perfect for fuss-free, everyday shooting.

Color filters

Each Fotomat comes with a range of Color Filters from which to pick to match your mood.

Wide-angle lens

Broaden your horizons and expand your view to 90° by attaching a Wide-angle lens. With an equivalent focal length of 21mm and a closest focusing distance of 0.3m, it’s perfect for capturing all your friends in a massive group selfie – available now as part of the Combo package!

Fisheye lens

See up to 170° and give your photos the nautical treatment by attaching a Fisheye lens. This wacky lens has a closest focusing distance of 10cm, so you can take super-sharp circular shots – available now as part of the Combo package!

Close-up lens

Attach a Close-up lens and shoot sharp and detailed shots from as close as 10cm – available now as part of the Combo package!
